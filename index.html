🚶 Movement & Controls

  • W A S D — move, Shift — run
  • Right Click (hold) or touch the screen — look around
  • E or the touch interact button — used for every interaction in the game
  • Number keys — quickly eat or use items in your inventory
  • Esc — close the open menu

🏗️ The Lighthouse

The tower has several floors connected by a rope ladder, each floor furnished with its own items like chairs, a bed, a bookshelf, a crafting table, a storage chest…

🍎 Hunger

Your stomach gets hungrier over time, and being too hungry slows you down. Eat food you've gathered to restore it.

🔥 Lighthouse Beacon & Oil

The beacon burns continuously and gradually uses up oil. Collect oil drums drifting at sea and bring them up to refuel the tank so the light doesn't go out.

🌙 Day & Night

The sky cycles through day and night, affecting the light and the sky. You can lie down on the bed to sleep, skipping part of the night and easing your hunger a little.

🛠️ Crafting

The crafting table lets you use gathered materials to make new items, like a bed, a storage chest, or a canoe to head out to sea.

📦 Storage Chest

Lets you store items from your inventory into the chest and take them back when needed, freeing up bag space.

🎣 Fishing

Hold the fishing rod, face the sea, and interact to cast your line. Time it right as a fish swims by to reel it in.

🚤 Canoe & Heading Out to Sea

Once you've crafted a canoe, you can sail out to open sea and collect drifting oil drums to resupply the lighthouse. Go far enough and you'll spot a small strange landmass out at sea — you can go ashore there and sail the canoe back later.

🏴‍☠️ Shipwreck — Rare Event

Occasionally a drifting shipwreck appears somewhere out at sea. Sail the canoe close and interact to search it before it drifts away.

⛵ Merchant Ship — Rare Event

Occasionally a merchant ship passes by near the lighthouse. Sail the canoe close and interact to trade before it sails out of sight.

🐑 Sheep Pen

Trade a merchant ship for a Lamb, then put it in the pen near the lighthouse. Feed it Rice once a day — after a few days of feeding it'll grow into a big sheep.

📻 Radio

The old radio lets you pick a few background songs to listen to while gazing at the sea.

💾 Save & Continue

The save button in the corner of the screen (or auto-save after certain actions) records your progress — position, items, time of day… Next time you open the game, you can choose Continue to pick up right where you left off, or New Game to start over.
